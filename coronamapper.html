KARNATAKA HOME QUARATINED PEOPLE GEOLOCATION DATA

This map consists of locations of the people who are home quarantined in all parts of Karnataka. The addresses of the home quarantined people are geotagged in the map.

The source of data is from a health ministry source and the accuracy of the locations can be considered to be 60%-70%
